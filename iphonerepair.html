Various iPhone Repairs

This is fairly self explanitory, I have experience with repairing a wide range of iOS devices. I think Apple makes great phones (if you ignore how fragile they are, their increasing dependance of serialized parts that make repairs impossible, the fact that they don't sell official parts, etc...)

I believe that our phones should serve us longer than a year or two, hence why I'm so big on repairing them. It keeps money in your pocket to not buy a new phone when your screen cracks on your current phone, and it's better for the environment when you keep devices out of landfills.
