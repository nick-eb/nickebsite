Modded Gameboy Color

This is a cool little project I did; it's a modded Gameboy Color with an IPS backlit screen & 2000mAh rechargable battery mod. It charges via micro USB and looks amazing and has even more amazing battery life, even though the screen draws more power. One battery benchmark I ran showed that this Gameboy can do over 8 hours of gameplay.

I used a FunnyPlaying IPS screen from their website, a generic 2000mAh battery from AliExpress and a TP4056 charging board. It's honestly a pretty simple and common mod these days, but I personally think it's really neat.
